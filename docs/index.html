Momentum — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

The short version: Momentum is private by default. If you never upgrade to Pro cloud sync, nothing you log ever leaves your phone.

What we collect

Free tier (default for everyone): None. All workout data — sessions, sets, plans, body weight, goals — is stored only in a local database on your device. We do not receive it, see it, or have any way to access it.

Pro tier (cloud sync, optional): If you create an account and enable cloud sync, we store the following on our own server so your data can sync across your devices:

  • Your email address (used only to identify your account)
  • Your workout data (plans, sessions, sets, body weight, goals) — the same data already stored locally on your device

We do not sell this data, share it with third parties, or use it for advertising. It exists solely so your own data can follow you across your own devices.

Payments

Subscription purchases are processed by RevenueCat and the Apple App Store / Google Play Store. We never see or store your payment card details. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for how they handle purchase data.

What we don't do

  • No advertising, no ad networks, no ad tracking
  • No third-party analytics SDKs
  • No selling or sharing of your data with data brokers

Your data, your control

  • Export: export all your data as CSV or JSON at any time from Settings, whether or not you're a Pro subscriber.
  • Delete: deleting your account removes your data from our server. Data already stored locally on your device is only removed by deleting the app.

Children's privacy

Momentum is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
